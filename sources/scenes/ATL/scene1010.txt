Yon Rha's mother says: "Yon Rha! You lazy piece of work, I need something."
Yon Rha says: "Yes, Mother. What is it?"
Yon Rha's mother says: "The tomato-carrots from your garden are too hard for my gums. I need something soft and juicy!"
Yon Rha says: "Would you like something else from the garden? I would be happy to ..."
Yon Rha's mother says: "Forget your stupid garden!  Get your grimy hind to the market and buy me some real food!"
